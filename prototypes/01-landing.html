What Is This

Treasure Box lets you upload photos of meaningful objects, attach stories and links to each one, and embed a physics-driven drawer widget anywhere on the web. Your items tumble realistically inside a drawer powered by a 2D physics engine, complete with collision sounds and AI-generated artwork for the drawer itself.

Think of it as a personal museum you can place on any webpage. Visitors pull the drawer open, watch your treasures spill out, long-press to read the story behind each item, and click through to whatever you've linked. It is a tangible, tactile way to share the things that matter to you.
